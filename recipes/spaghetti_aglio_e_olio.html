Sphaghetti Aglio e Olio

Description

An easy and flavorful Italian pasta dish featuring spaghetti tossed in a garlic-infused olive oil, with a hint of red pepper flakes for a subtle kick. Garnished with fresh parsley and grated Parmesan cheese, this dish is a quick and satisfying option for any pasta lover.

Ingredients:

  • 400g spaghetti
  • 1/2 cup olive oil
  • 6 cloves garlic, thinly sliced
  • 1 teaspoon red pepper flakes (adjust to taste)
  • Salt and black pepper to taste
  • 1/4 cup chopped fresh parsley
  • Grated Parmesan cheese for serving

Steps:

  1. Cook spaghetti according to package instructions. Reserve a cup of pasta water before draining.
  2. In a large skillet, heat olive oil over medium heat. Add sliced garlic and red pepper flakes. Saut√© until garlic is golden but not browned.
  3. Add cooked spaghetti to the skillet, tossing to coat in the garlic-infused oil. If needed, add a bit of the reserved pasta water to create a light sauce.
  4. Season with salt and black pepper to taste. Toss in chopped parsley.
  5. Serve hot, garnished with grated Parmesan cheese.
