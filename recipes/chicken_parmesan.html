Chicken Parmesan

Description:

A classic Italian-American dish featuring tender and seasoned chicken breasts, coated in a crispy breadcrumb and Parmesan crust, baked to perfection with marinara sauce and melted mozzarella. Garnished with fresh basil for a burst of flavor.

Ingredients:

  • 4 boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • large eggs, beaten
  • 1 cup breadcrumbs
  • 1 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 2 cups marinara sauce
  • 1 cup shredded mozzarella cheese
  • Fresh basil for garnish

Steps:

  1. Preheat oven to 400°F (200°C).
  2. Season chicken breasts with salt and pepper.
  3. Dredge each chicken breast in flour, dip in beaten eggs, and coat with a mixture of breadcrumbs and Parmesan cheese. In a large skillet, heat olive oil over medium-high heat.
  4. Cook chicken until golden brown on both sides.
  5. Place the cooked chicken in a baking dish, top with marinara sauce and mozzarella cheese.
  6. Bake in the preheated oven for 20 minutes or until the cheese is melted and bubbly.
  7. Garnish with fresh basil before serving.
